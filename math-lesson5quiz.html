Fractions (Basic) Quiz

1. Which fraction represents one-half?

2. In the fraction 3/4, what does the 4 represent?

3. Which fraction shows the shaded part of this circle? (Imagine 1 of 4 parts is shaded)

4. Which is equal to 1/2?

5. If you cut a pizza into 8 equal slices and eat 2 slices, what fraction of the pizza have you eaten?

6. What fraction of this set is shaded? (Imagine 3 out of 5 circles shaded)

7. On a number line from 0 to 1, where would 1/4 be located?

8. Which fraction is NOT equivalent to 1/2?

9. If you fold a paper in half and then in half again, each piece is what fraction of the whole?

10. Three friends share 2 candy bars equally. What fraction of a candy bar does each friend get?
